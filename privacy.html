Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Anonymous Usage Data: We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous statistics about how visitors use our website, including page views, time spent on pages, and general geographic location (country/region level).
  • Technical Information: Browser type, device information, and IP address (anonymized).
  • User Preferences: Theme preferences and language settings stored locally in your browser.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Understand which features are most useful to visitors
  • Analyze traffic patterns and optimize content
  • Fix bugs and technical issues

Data Sharing

We only share data with:

  • Google Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics for analysis purposes
  • No Third Parties: We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with other companies

Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies for:

  • Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics cookies to track website usage (only with your consent)
  • Preference Cookies: To remember your theme and language preferences
  • Consent Cookies: To remember your cookie preferences
Your Control: You can accept or decline analytics cookies at any time using the cookie consent banner or the "Manage Cookies" link in the footer.

Data Retention

We retain data for the following periods:

  • Analytics Data: Google Analytics retains data for 26 months by default
  • Cookie Consent: Your consent preference is stored for 1 year
  • Local Preferences: Theme and language preferences are stored indefinitely in your browser's local storage

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Accept or decline analytics tracking
  • Change your cookie preferences at any time
  • Clear your browser's cookies and local storage
  • Use our website without analytics tracking

Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • No storage of personally identifiable information on our servers
  • IP address anonymization in Google Analytics
  • Regular security reviews and updates

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

International Users

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages and may be accessed from anywhere in the world. By using our website, you consent to the transfer and processing of your information in accordance with this privacy policy.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us through our GitHub repository.
