About Me

I was born and grew up in the countryside of Dong Nai province - Dinh Quan District. I am not good at anything and do not have any unique talents, but I am only passionately curious. My parents expected me to study at a top university in Ho Chi Minh city, but I cannot do so. Studying at Dalat university was a good choice for me, but my parents did not think so. Therefore I suffered many pressures in my early days at Dalat University. To make my parents happy, I had gotten many university scholarships to cover the living cost and tuition. I have just graduated excellent Engineering Degree from Dalat University on 26th March 2021. I promise to do my best in the lecturer role at Dalat University.
